EKHO Sigiriya, is located in a destination like no other, featuring tales from a bygone era of lavish kingdoms and structural wonders surrounded by a picturesque backdrop of tropical paradise. This fascinating structure is also recognized as a UNESCO world heritage site. The comfort of the rooms knows no bounds. Designed to mirror the bygone architecture of a Sri Lankan home all rooms denote the craftsmanship of local talent. With most of the rooms overlooking the captivating Sigiriya Rock Fortress, EKHO Sigiriya ensures your private space within the kingdom.
