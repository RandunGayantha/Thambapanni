Come and stay at Radisson Blu Resort, Galle and enjoy our stunning seaside location. Take a dip in our outdoor pool, the social hub connecting our hotel to outlets and the ocean. Relax in one of our 172 modern and stylish rooms, each equipped with a private balcony or terrace and thoughtful amenities such as free Wi-Fi and coffee and tea making facilities. Perfect for families, our Kids Club provides multiple activities for little guests as well as a kid's pool in the outdoor pool area. Unique to our hotel and only available with us, the Lounge is an area with lockers for luggage storage, toilets, and showers to facilitate early check-ins or late departures for your convenience. Enjoy a delicious meal or a light snack at one of our four available on-site restaurants and rooftop bar serving a range of fresh local delicacies.
