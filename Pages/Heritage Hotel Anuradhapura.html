Heritage Hotel is located in the city center of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It offers rooms with cable TV, an outdoor pool, 2 dining options and free parking. Free Wi-Fi access is available at all areas. Rooms at the Heritage Hotel come with a mini-bar, private bathroom and balcony. Business facilities, laundry and currency exchange services are available. Guests can dine at the restaurant or enjoy light snacks and beverages at the bar. Heritage Hotel is 3 hour, 30 minutes drive from Bandaranaike International Airport. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.2 for a two-person trip. Surrounded by trees and overlooking Tissa Wewa reservoir, this casual hotel is 4 km from Anuradhapura New Town railway station and 6 km from Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi temple. Warm, understated rooms feature balconies with reservoir or garden views, and offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, along with rainfall showers, and tea and coffeemaking equipment. Amenities include an international restaurant, a sports bar, and an outdoor pool surrounded by tropical trees, plus a poolside bar.
