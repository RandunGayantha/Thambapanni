Situated within 1.5 km of Milagiriya Beach and 1.8 km of Wellawatte Beach, Havelock Place Bungalow features rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Colombo. This bed and breakfast provides accommodation with a patio. The bed and breakfast offers parking on-site, an outdoor swimming pool and full-day security. Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, units at the bed and breakfast also boast free WiFi. At the bed and breakfast, every unit is fitted with bed linen and towels. At the bed and breakfast, the modern restaurant is open for dinner and specialises in local cuisine. Guests can also relax in the garden. Bambalapitiya Beach is 2 km from Havelock Place Bungalow, while Bambalapitiya Railway Station is 2.6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ratmalana International, 9 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
