Located within 1.1 km of Kada Panaha Tank and 1.8 km of Nature Park Anuradhapura in the centre of Anuradhapura, DELMOND Lake - Anuradhapura offers accommodation with seating area. The air-conditioned accommodation is 1.4 km from Kumbichchan Kulama Tank. There is free private parking and the property provides paid airport shuttle service. With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, units at the guest house also provide guests with free WiFi, while certain rooms here will provide you with a terrace. At the guest house, the units are equipped with bed linen and towels. Guests can also relax in the garden. Anuradhapura Railway Station is 3.1 km from the guest house, while Attikulama Tank is 3.6 km away. The nearest airport is Sigiriya Airport, 68 km from DELMOND Lake - Anuradhapura.
Boasting a private entrance, this air-conditioned apartment includes 1 living room, 2 separate bedrooms and 1 bathroom with a bath and a shower. The apartment has a seating area, a dining area, a sofa as well as a terrace with lake views. The unit has 3 beds.
