Set in Kitulgala and with Nawalapitiya Railway Station reachable within 30 km, Moksha at Kitulgala-Rainforest Boutique Hotel offers express check-in and check-out, allergy-free rooms, a restaurant, free WiFithroughout the property and an outdoor swimming pool. All guest rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bath, bathrobes and slippers. At Moksha at Kitulgala - Rainforest Boutique Hotel all rooms are fitted with bed linen and towels. The accommodation offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Moksha at Kitulgala - Rainforest Boutique Hotel. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.5 for a two-person trip.
