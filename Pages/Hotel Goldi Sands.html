Goldi Sands is a Tourist Hotel located right on the soft sandy beach amidst the swaying coconut palms of Negombo and only 15 minutes drive from the Bandaranaike International Airport.. Accommodation at Goldi Sands comprises a stunning array of 131 air-conditioned double rooms, which includes Deluxe Rooms, Standard rooms, Suites (Duplex), Junior Suites and Economy rooms. All rooms has a private balcony / terrace with a view of the Indian Ocean, except for the Economy rooms. Already a well known established property, Goldi Sands Hotel is famous for its constantly excellent service and attention to detail. What better to accompany our culinary delights than a bottle or two of the fine wine from our well-stocked wine cellar over a game of pool? Our cozy bar provides a wide variety from coolers and cocktails to lager and liquor. We warmly welcome you to Goldi Sands Hotel assuring you of our best services, personal attention and a memorable stay
