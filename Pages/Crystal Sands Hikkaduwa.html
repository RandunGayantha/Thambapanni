Crystal Sands Hikkaduwa is located in Hikkaduwa, just a few feet from Rathgama Beach. It offers accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center, and a garden. This motel features a restaurant, room service, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi throughout. Guests can make use of the bar. The hotel will serve visitors with air-conditioned rooms that include a closet, a coffee machine, a fridge, an oven, a safe deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a patio, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Crystal Sands Hikkaduwa provides some rooms with city views, and each one has a balcony. The rooms contain bed linen and towels. Breakfast is offered every morning and comprises Continental, Full English/Irish and American. The neighborhood is known for cycling, and this 5-star hotel provides automobile rental services. Crystal Sands Hikkaduwa is located 700 metres from Boossa Beach and 2 kilometers from Dodanduwa Beach. The nearest airport is Koggala Airport, which is 24 kilometers from the hotel.
