Royal Botanic Gardens, Peradeniya

Botanical garden in Kandy

It is near the Mahaweli River (The longest river in Sri Lanka). It is renowned for its collection of orchids. The garden includes more than 4000 species of plants, including orchids, spices, medicinal plants and palm trees. Attached to it is the "National Herbarium of Sri Lanka. What is the primary role of the botanic gardens? Our main activity is to conserve plants and provide opportunities for the public to study, sustainably conserve, and admire plant resources in natural and manmade environments.
