Located in Ella, within 5.3 km of Demodara Nine Arch Bridge and 49 km of Hakgala Botanical Garden, Lavendra Paradise provides accommodation with a garden and free WiFi as well as free private parking for guests who drive. The property is set 50 km from Horton Plains National Park, 1.3 km from Ella Spice Garden and 1 km from Ella Railway Station. The accommodation offers room service and a 24-hour front desk for guests. All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. At the inn, every room comes with a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The area is popular for hiking, and bike hire and car hire are available at Lavendra Paradise. Ella Rock is 2.8 km from the accommodation, while Little Adam's Peak is 3 km away. The nearest airport is Mattala Rajapaksa International, 83 km from Lavendra Paradise, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
