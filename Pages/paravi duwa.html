Pravi Duwa

Matara Pravi Duwa

Paravi Duwa, located in Matara, Sri Lanka, is a small yet captivating island that holds significant cultural and historical value. Nestled in the heart of the southern coast, this picturesque islet is accessible by a charming footbridge, offering visitors a serene escape from the bustling mainland. Paravi Duwa is home to a beautiful Buddhist temple, which stands as a testament to the island's rich religious heritage. The temple, surrounded by lush greenery and the gentle lapping of ocean waves, provides a tranquil setting for meditation and reflection. The island's scenic beauty, coupled with its spiritual ambiance, makes Paravi Duwa a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking peace and rejuvenation.
